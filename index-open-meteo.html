TalviFox's Weather Widget

This is a custom, lightweight, and dynamic weather widget built by TalviFox.

Acknowledgments & Data Sources

A huge shout out to the projects and services making this possible:

  • Open-Meteo: Supplying lightning-fast, globally available weather forecasts with a completely free and open API.
  • Weather Underground: Providing the hyper-local fallback weather links.
  • NASA: Providing beautiful astronomical imagery for our accurate moon phases!

Developer Notes

This Open-Meteo version works globally for any latitude and longitude! Unlike the NWS version, it does not require a strict User-Agent, but it's always good practice to include one if you are running this from a server-side wrapper.
