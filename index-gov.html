TalviFox's Weather Widget

This is a custom, lightweight, and dynamic weather widget built by TalviFox.

Acknowledgments & Data Sources

A huge shout out to the projects and services making this possible:

  • National Weather Service (NWS): Providing the core forecasting data and grid points.
  • NASA: Providing beautiful astronomical imagery for our accurate moon phases!

Developer Notes

Important: When integrating this widget into your own applications, remember to set a descriptive User-Agent header in your requests. The NWS requires a unique User-Agent (like your app name and contact email) to prevent rate limiting and ensure stable access to their API.
